About Us

A boutique built for exceptional clients

JeffMon Alternative Investments is a Dubai-based asset management firm founded by Mohammad Ali Jaffery and Murtaza Memon, CFA, built to deliver bespoke discretionary and advisory investment strategies across global markets.

We work with discerning individuals and family offices who want more than off-the-shelf solutions. At JeffMon, portfolios aren't slotted into risk buckets — they are built from scratch, shaped by research, tailored to each client's goals, and rigorously managed across markets.

Our ecosystem of founders, strategic backers, and institutional advisors allows us to combine boutique-level agility with institutional-grade discipline; delivering performance, protection, and partnership that lasts.

Tailored & Flexible

Bespoke portfolios designed from scratch to match your objectives and risk profile — with tactical flexibility to scale exposure up or down as markets shift.

Selective Conviction

Aligned & Informed

Investment Process

Anticipate. Balance. Select.

We provide strategic multi-asset investment advice combining global macro foresight with deep, bottom-up research. Every position is chosen for a reason; every portfolio is built for the client.

01
Determine Economic Trends

The investment team first meets to analyze the macro-economical situation and determine relevant market trends, positioning 2–3 years ahead of consensus.

02
Asset Allocation Decisions

Based on the macro environment, we decide asset allocation across asset classes, geographies and sectors with secular tailwinds.

03
Screening Process

Once sectors are identified, rigorous fundamental research begins to screen and select potential winners within each sector.

04
Evaluate Company Fundamentals

Our research team analyzes company financials, 10-Ks, 10-Qs, and earnings calls to understand the direction of each company under consideration.

05
Create Proprietary Model

Analysts create a proprietary model for each company under review to forecast its performance and perform valuation exercises.

06
Final Selection & Investment Decision

Final selection is made by the analyst and presented to the investment committee where the thesis and financials go through a rigorous vetting process before any capital is deployed.
